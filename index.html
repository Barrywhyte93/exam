African School Certificate Examination (WASSCE) for School Candidatesk 2022

BOOKKEEPING 1

Objective Test

Do not open this booklet until you are told to do so. While you are waiting, write your name and index number in the spaces provided at the top right-hand corner of this booklet and thereafter, read the following instructions carefully.

  1. Use HB pencil throughout
  2. if you have got a blank answer sheet, complete its top section as follows.
    1. In the space marked Name, write in capital letters your surname followed by other names.
    2. In the spaces marked Examination, Year, Subject and Paper, write 'WASSCE (SC)', 2022', 'BOOKKEEPING' and '1' respectively.
    3. In the box marked Index Number, write your index number vertically in the spaces on the left-hand side. There are numbered spaces in line with each digit. Shade carefully the space with the same number as each digit.
    4. In the box marked Paper Code, write the digits 109112 in the spaces on the left-hand side. Shade the corresponding nmbered spaces in the same way as for your index number.
    5. In the box marked Sex, shade the space marked M if you are a male, or F if you are a female.
  3. If you have got a pre-printed answer sheet, check that the details are correctly printed, as described in 2 above. In the boxes marked Index Number, Paper Code and Sex, reshade each of the shaded spaces.
